About Me

My name is Rhojealyn Ann Rizo. I am currently studying Bachelor of Arts in Communication. Communication plays an important role in society because it connects people, spreads information, and builds understanding among communities.

As a student of communication, I am interested in digital media, storytelling, and how technology influences the way people interact online. Modern communication is no longer limited to television, radio, or newspapers. Today, social media platforms and digital technology allow messages to reach millions of people instantly.

Career Goals

In the future, I aim to become a responsible communication professional who uses technology ethically. I want to create content that informs, inspires, and promotes truthful communication. I believe we all have a responsibility to use technology in ways that respect human dignity meaning being honest in every action or avoiding actions that may harm others, and thinking about the long-term impact of our decisions.
