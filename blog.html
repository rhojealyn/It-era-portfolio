Blog: Communication in the Digital Age

Technology has greatly changed the way people communicate. In the past, communication was mostly done through letters, newspapers, radio, and television. Today, digital technology allows people to communicate instantly through social media platforms, messaging applications, and online websites.

Because of this rapid development, communication students must learn how to use digital tools responsibly. The spread of information is very fast, which means that misinformation can also spread easily if people are not careful.

Living in the IT Era requires digital literacy. People must learn how to verify information, respect privacy, and communicate responsibly online.
